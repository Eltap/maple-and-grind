Our Story

Maple & Grind opened in 2019 with one idea: a coffee shop should feel like an extension of your living room. No rush, no pretense — just good coffee and a place to slow down.

We roast in small batches with a partner just down the street, bake everything in-house before sunrise, and try to remember your name by your third visit.
